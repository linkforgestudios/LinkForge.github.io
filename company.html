About Us

LinkForge Studios was founded in 2023 with a simple love for pixel art and the magic of interactive storytelling. What began as small sketches and late-night ideas quickly grew into a vision: to create games that feel as personal as they are playful. From the very beginning, our goal has been to blend creativity and craft, making experiences that not only entertain but also linger in memory.

We believe games are more than just mechanics and visuals. They are journeys, stories, and emotions woven together through interaction. That belief drives us to approach each project with care and imagination. Whether it's experimenting with unusual ideas, refining every detail, or testing how players connect to a moment, we treat development as both an art and a dialogue.

At LinkForge, community is central to everything we do. We listen closely to feedback, invite collaboration, and share our progress openly. By staying connected with players, we ensure that our games grow alongside the people who experience them. This relationship keeps our work authentic and helps us create worlds that truly resonate.

Our philosophy is simple: quality over speed, passion over trends. We don't chase shortcuts or rush projects; instead, we focus on building games that feel alive and meaningful. Every title is a chance to experiment, learn, and push ourselves further, while still staying true to the spirit that started it all.
